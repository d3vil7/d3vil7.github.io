We Value Your Data

μNEST is an IoT + Blockchain project built by a group of geeks who have 15yr+ experience in applied crypto and electronics industry.

It provides not only a pub-blockchain but also a low-level hardware platform for developers to explore a brand new world.

We ensure it secure, we make it simple!

Unique Features

IoT is data, data means everything.
Instead of reinventing consensus algorithm, DPoS is employed.

01

μWallet

A secure hardware wallet with the EAL5+ SE, can be regarded as an entrance to blockchain and be used in M2M payment.

02

μGate

A multi-protocol and multi-function gateway playing the key role both for IoT and blockchain.

03

nVM

A lightweight VM with a programmer friendly high-level language interface providing containers for smart contracts.

04

μZone

Combining hardware and Layer2 technology like Lightning Network, it solves the TPS problem and enhances availability of the blockchain.

μNEST : An IoT + Blockchain Project

Embarking from the angle of resolving existing practical problems of realizing IoT + blockchain application scenarios, integrating our solid applied cryptography knowledge and rich experience in electronics industry, μNEST core team figures out our own unique solutions.

Our focus is to solve the security issues related to IoT Deployment, Upgrade and Operation. We also commit to address the purity, integrity, and privacy of IoT data in its whole life cycle. And we strive to guarantee M2M interaction securely and friendly.

Only with trusted data, connecting the world makes it more meaningful for you and me.
